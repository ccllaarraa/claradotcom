Bernadeta Rimutyte IGWEB
Where do the ideas for your illustrations come from, any specific references you have?

My illustrations are all about something I overheard, said, noticed, experienced, and witnessed in daily life. I enjoy observing people, animals, and situations.

I tend to memorize strange and peculiar settings that inspire me, which could be real life, movies, podcasts, dreams. Usually, it translates into an odd-looking character and a certain word combination. My purest ideas and inspiration come when I'm very tired and true to myself.
Are you currently developing or working on something new?

I am currently working on a few animal-themed paintings. One of them has the soul of a wolf, the other souls of squirrels.
